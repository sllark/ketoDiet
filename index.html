What is a Smart Pill?

Smart Pills or nootropics go by different names. They are called brain boosters, brain enhancement pills, memory enhancing pills and so on. These smart pills are composed of ingredients which are known to have a positive effect on brain function. Smart pill manufacturers add a variety of natural ingredients, plant extracts, and synthetically derived natural compounds to their products which provide a positive boost to brain functioning.

Nootropics are known to have encouraging results on memory, focus, thought clarity, and overall mood and wellbeing. Nootropics are designed to be used by a broad segment of the population ranging from students, working adults, and even the elderly.

Best Ingredients for An Effective Nootropic

A vast number of ingredients are used in making good quality brain boosters. Many natural herbs have been used since ancient times in many cultures which are known to improve cognitive skills. Examples of such herbs are Ginko Biloba, Bacopa Monnieri, St. John’s Wort, etc.

Similarly, there are many compounds which are found in our body which help in maintaining brain function. Many of these compounds like Phosphatidylserine, Acetyl L-Carnitine, Tyrosine, Theanine, etc are synthesized and added to Smart Pill complexes to better its functioning. Some of the most common ingredients of memory enhancing pills are:

  • Bacopa

    Bacopa Monnieri is a herb which has been used in traditional Indian healing for hundreds of years. Bacopa has positives effect on memory and focus and it improves long-term memory recall. It is a staple of a high-grade brain health supplement

  • Ginko

    Ginko is an ancient nootropic herb which helps in improving memory, concentration boost and maintains energy levels.

  • St John’s Wort

    It is a known herb to have calming effects and reduces anxiety which leads to a better mood.

  • Cat’s Claw

    Cat’s claw (Uncaria tomentosa) is a vine that grows in the Amazon Rainforest. It is believed to have neuroprotective benefits and can improve your mental performance and is increasingly used in memory enhancer pills.

  • Rhodiola Rosea

    It is an herb which is known to increase energy levels, endurance, strength, and mental capacity.

  • Alpha GPC

    This component increases the amount of Acetylcholine present in the brain, thereby improving concentration, memory, and speed of learning. Alpha GPC is also touted to be helpful for treating Alzheimer’s disease

  • Huperzine A

    Huperzine A helps in reducing Acetylcholinesterase, a compound which prevents the action of Acetylcholine. This ultimately stimulates brain function and improve mental clarity.

  • Phosphatidylserine

    It is a very effective ingredient in smart drugs that help in improving cognitive function, memory, focus, and attention. The chemical enhances neurotransmission by acting as a phospholipid within the brain and helps in cognitive enhancement.

  • Theanine

    This is a compound in tea and it is known for its calming benefits. It helps in reducing stress and helps increase focus.

  • Tyrosine

    Tyrosine is known for its role in helping concentration and focus, especially in stressful moments.

It is not necessary for memory enhancing pills to have all of these ingredients. Some brain boosters have many other ingredients which act in synergy with other ingredients to deliver a better experience. Many manufacturers continuously develop newer ingredients which have better effects than their counterparts.
